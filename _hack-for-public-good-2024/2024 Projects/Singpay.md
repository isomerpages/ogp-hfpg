<h3>Singpay</h3>
<h4>Problem</h4>
<p>It is common for individuals to lose track of payments or forget to pay
bills.</p>
<h4>Solution</h4>
<p>Singpay is a platform that enables individuals to view upcoming bills
and payments made to government agencies, thereby improving their visibility
of their payment transactions. Users can log in with Singpass to see all
bills issued to them. Click <a href="https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1ReP_Hyi0wX8uZoqm0GQSq146Si1xuPC7w9wH6YStrvM/edit#slide=id.g2684c7e4628_0_0" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">here</a> to
read more.</p>
