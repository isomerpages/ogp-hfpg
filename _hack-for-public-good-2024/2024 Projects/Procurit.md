<h3>Procurit</h3>
<h4>Problem </h4>
<p>We believe that optimizing procurement procedures is essential for saving
valuable time, resources, and effort, while effectively reducing costs
associated with delays and missed opportunities.</p>
<h4>Solution</h4>
<p>Procurit is a web-based application designed to streamline procurement
processes for public officers while upholding strict adherence to procurement
regulations and principles.</p>
<p>Procurit offers a foundational workflow for procurement that can be tailored
to meet the specific needs of different agencies. Current features include
simplified creation of SVP &amp; DC Procurement Requests, streamlined Approval
Routing, instant Email Notifications, comprehensive Request Tracking, and
detailed Audit Trails.</p>
