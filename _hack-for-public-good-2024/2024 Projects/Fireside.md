<h2>Fireside</h2>
<h4>Problem</h4>
<p>More young adults are facing work burnout. 57% of young adults felt that
burnout affected their mental health.</p>
<h4>Solution</h4>
<p>Fireside is a webapp that tackles the growing problem of work burnout
among young working adults. It enables you to:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Assess your work stressors</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Share your work rants anonymously and securely</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Convert work rants into constructive feedback for managers and HR</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Explore self-help resources to manage work burnout symptoms</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Flamey, our bot mascot, is guided by the Cognitive Behavioural Therapy
(CBT) framework, which aims to identify and challenge negative thought
patterns and behaviour to promote healthier cognitive and emotional responses.</p>
<p>Check out the prototype <a href="https://go.gov.sg/hfpg-fireside" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">here</a>. For any queries
or collaborations, email us at <a href="https://go.gov.sg/hfpg-fireside" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank"><u>fireside@open.gov.sg</u></a>
</p>
