<h4>Problem</h4>
<p>Singapore has been facing dwindling volunteerism rates, from 35% in 2016
to 22% in 2021, largely due 3 key factors:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p><strong>Existing platforms </strong>are not volunteer-centric with zero
volunteer profiling nor retention</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Today’s citizens</strong> are purpose &amp; goal-oriented, with
strong “What’s in it for me” mindsets</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Opportunity organisers </strong>lack resources and knowledge to
better engage volunteers</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h4>Solution</h4>
<p>For citizens, ServeSG eliminates the usual barriers to volunteering, such
as unreliable account creation, lengthy registration forms, and most importantly,
the difficulty in finding opportunities that align with your numerous requirements.</p>

<h4>Impact</h4>
<p>The end goal for ServeSG is to nurture a Singapore where every citizen
is a volunteer. We believe ServeSG, in doing so, will elevate the lives
of those living in Singapore, beneficiary and benefactor alike.</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>For Singapore,</strong> we build stronger communities that connect
to care</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>For beneficiaries,</strong> get the help you need to live better</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>For individuals,</strong> achieve your personal goals, be it skill
development, improved well-being, sharpened social skills, etc</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>In doing so, ServeSG simultaneously supports opportunity organisers by
streamlining end-to-end volunteer management processes, allowing you to
save time, money, and manpower.</p>
