<h3><strong>AskHer</strong></h3>
<h4>Problem</h4>
<p>Many women struggle to find healthcare professionals that deeply empathise
with their reproductive health issues.</p>
<h4>Solution</h4>
<p>Through AskHer, we want to make information on women’s health and women’s
healthcare providers more accessible, transparent, and useful for women
in Singapore. We aim to do this via a comprehensive public online platform
that provides verified reviews on public clinics and well-written content
that empowers women to truly take control of their reproductive health.</p>
